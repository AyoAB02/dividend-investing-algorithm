# Dividend Investing Algorithm

A quantitative dividend stock screener that identifies the top 10 income-generating securities by analysing dividend yield, payout sustainability, and growth history.

## Overview

This algorithm evaluates securities based on their income-generating characteristics, combining current yield with sustainability and growth metrics to identify reliable dividend payers. The scoring system balances high current income against long-term dividend sustainability.

## Methodology

Securities are scored across:
- **Dividend Yield** — current annual dividend relative to share price
- **Payout Ratio** — proportion of earnings distributed as dividends (sustainability indicator)
- **Dividend Growth History** — consistency and rate of dividend increases over time

## Tools & Libraries

- **Python** (pandas, NumPy, yfinance, SciPy)
- Dividend and fundamental data via Yahoo Finance API
- Statistical ranking and composite scoring

## Key Outputs

- Ranked list of top 10 income-generating securities
- Composite dividend quality scores
- Yield vs. sustainability analysis
- Recommended position sizes based on portfolio value
